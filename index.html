INTRODUCTION
Founded in 1973 on three basic principles, Patco continues to serve its customers today with the same Pride, Quality and Commitment that has been there since the beginning.

When John R. Kilpatrick founded Patco in November of 1973, his plan was to operate as a machine shop, specializing in pins, shafts, bushings and other oilfield related machined parts. During the downturn of the 1980’s, Patco expanded its product base and facilities. This marked the beginning of the value added focus that Patco is known for today. Through continued investment in key people and critical equipment, Patco is now your trusted source for Engineered Turnkey Solutions.
