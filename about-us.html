Frequently Asked Questions

What does Engineered Turnkey Solutions mean?

Engineered Turnkey Solutions means that Patco can assist you during any phase of your project, or we can manage your entire project, start to finish, from design to delivery.

Do you have a Quality Management System?

Yes. Patco is ISO 9001:2015 certified and conducts bi-annual reviews to make sure our process guarantees our customers the highest level of quality.

What kind of manufacturing services do you offer?

Patco has CNC machining capabilities, as well as several ABS & DNV approved welding procedures. In addition to machining and fabrication, we have an entire department devoted to assembly, hydro-testing, flushing, electrical, load testing, instrumentation and more.

Will you work with me to jointly design a product to meet my project needs?

Yes! Patco's engineers and designers can assist you with developing your product. We can also manufacture and test the prototype for you once the design is finalized.

Can you support my project overseas or offshore?

Yes. Patco has field service technicians available to travel globally to support your project needs. Visit our Field Service & Support page for more info.

By allowing Patco to supply your project needs turnkey, you can ensure your customer's requirements will be met and your bottom line will improve.
